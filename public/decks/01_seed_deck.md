### Deep Dive: Why Blockchain

# AI agents need money. Blockchain is the only way to give it to them.

AI is Internet native. Blockchains are Internet native financial rails.

**THE PROBLEM:** You can't load cash into an AI agent. Traditional financial systems have no API for autonomous software. Banks don't give accounts to algorithms.

**THE SOLUTION:** You can load a wallet with stablecoins. Agents are already trading, hedging, buying insurance — autonomously. Programmable money for programmable agents.
