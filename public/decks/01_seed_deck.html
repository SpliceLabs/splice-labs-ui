06.1 · WHY BLOCKCHAIN

AI agents need money. Blockchain is the only way to give it to them.

AI is Internet native. Blockchains are Internet native financial rails.

THE PROBLEM
You can't load cash into an AI agent
Traditional financial systems have no API for autonomous software. Banks don't give accounts to algorithms.
THE SOLUTION
You can load a wallet with stablecoins
Agents are already trading, hedging, buying insurance — autonomously. Programmable money for programmable agents.
REGULATORY TAILWIND
GENIUS Act + state stablecoin laws = regulatory green light for governed autonomous finance.
THE FUTURE
By 2027, more agents will transact on chain than humans. We're building the governance layer.
