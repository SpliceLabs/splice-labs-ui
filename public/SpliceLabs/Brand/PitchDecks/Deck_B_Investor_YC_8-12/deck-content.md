## Slide 2 — Problem

### On-Slide Copy
**Onchain systems are becoming autonomous — and the infrastructure isn't ready.**

1. **Retail-grade protocols** — Built fast, break under institutional scrutiny. No compliance posture, no audit depth, no cross-chain coherence.
2. **TradFi bolt-ons** — Legacy finance wrapping old systems in tokens. No protocol-native thinking, no agent compatibility, no composability.
3. **No one builds the hard middle** — Institutional-grade, protocol-native, agent-ready infrastructure that works across chains.

The result: serious capital sits on the sideline. Institutions want in, but the infrastructure doesn't exist.

### Speaker Notes
- Frame the problem as a market gap, not a complaint about existing players.
- "Retail-grade" means protocols that can't survive institutional due diligence.
- The punchline: serious capital is waiting for serious infrastructure.
