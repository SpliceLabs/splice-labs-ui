## Slide 4 — How We Deliver

### On-Slide Copy
**Our process.**

```
Discovery → Design → Build → Audit → Deploy → Operate
```

1. **Discovery** (2–4 weeks) — Requirements, constraints, economic modeling.
2. **Design** (4–6 weeks) — Architecture, tokenomics, mechanism specification.
3. **Build** (8–16 weeks) — Smart contracts, agent logic, integration.
4. **Audit** (4–6 weeks) — Third-party audit coordination and remediation.
5. **Deploy** (2–4 weeks) — Multi-chain deployment, monitoring setup.
6. **Operate** (ongoing) — HELIOS agent management, upgrades, incident response.

### Speaker Notes
- Walk through the timeline. Clients want predictability.
- Timelines are indicative — adjust for scope in conversation.
- Emphasize: we stay post-launch. Most studios disappear after deploy.
- Audit coordination is a value-add — we manage the audit relationship.
