# DECK D — SPEAKER NOTES (ALL SLIDES)

## Slide 1 — Title
- Client-facing: lead with what they get, not who we are.
- This slide should take 10 seconds.

## Slide 2 — What We Do
- Clients want to know: what exactly will you do for us?
- Walk through the list. Each bullet is a deliverable category.
- HELIOS is the differentiator — mention but don't over-explain here.

## Slide 3 — Who We Serve
- Match the client in the room to their category.
- The message: we've done this before for teams like yours.

## Slide 4 — How We Deliver
- Walk through the timeline. Clients want predictability.
- Emphasize: we stay post-launch. Most studios disappear after deploy.
- Audit coordination is a value-add.

## Slide 5 — HELIOS: Your Agent Layer
- This is the "why us" slide. HELIOS separates us from every other dev shop.
- Frame as ongoing value: you get an operating system, not just a build.
- Address trust: "You own the protocol. HELIOS operates under your rules."

## Slide 6 — Proof: What We've Built
- Let the work speak. Concrete outcomes per project.
- Use testimonials if available.
- Point to appendix case studies for depth.

## Slide 7 — Engagement Model
- Have a ballpark ready. Don't say "it depends" without a range.
- Hybrid is usually the right answer.
- Be prepared to discuss payment structure.

## Slide 8 — Next Steps
- End with a clear, low-friction next step.
- Have scheduling link ready. Don't make them email you.
